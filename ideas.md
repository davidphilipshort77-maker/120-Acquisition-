# 120% Business Acquisition Toolkit — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Executive Command Centre
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Bauhaus meets Bloomberg Terminal — structured authority

**Core Principles:**
- Precision grid with asymmetric column weights (sidebar + content)
- Information density balanced with generous whitespace
- Every element earns its place — no decoration without function
- Dark navy foundation with gold accents signals institutional credibility

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0D1B2A) as the dominant surface; warm gold (#C9A84C) as the sole accent; crisp white for data readability. The palette evokes private banking and top-tier M&A advisory — not a startup.

**Layout Paradigm:** Fixed left sidebar navigation (unit-based), wide content area with a persistent progress tracker at the top. Each tool opens as a full-width panel with structured sections.

**Signature Elements:**
- Ruled horizontal dividers with gold pip markers
- Monospaced numerals for financial data
- Subtle grain texture on the dark sidebar

**Interaction Philosophy:** Deliberate, confident transitions. No bouncy animations. Slide-in panels, fade-on-scroll reveals.

**Animation:** 200ms ease-out transitions; no spring physics; content fades in from 8px below.

**Typography System:** DM Serif Display (headings) + IBM Plex Mono (data/numbers) + Inter (body). Hierarchy: 48px / 32px / 20px / 14px.
</text>

## Approach 2: Architectural Blueprint
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Style — grid discipline, typographic rigour

**Core Principles:**
- Strict 12-column grid with visible structure
- Typography as the primary visual element
- Colour used sparingly — almost monochromatic with single accent
- Horizontal rule system creates rhythm

**Color Philosophy:** Off-white (#F8F6F1) background; charcoal (#1A1A1A) text; a single warm gold (#C9A84C) for interactive elements. Inspired by legal documents and investment prospectuses.

**Layout Paradigm:** Top navigation bar with unit tabs; content flows in a two-column editorial layout. Tool cards are structured like legal clauses — numbered, hierarchical.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large section numbers in light grey
- Thin border rules separating content zones
- Stamp-like "CRITICAL" / "HIGH" / "MEDIUM" priority badges

**Interaction Philosophy:** Keyboard-first navigation; clean accordion expansions; no hover gimmicks.

**Animation:** Minimal — only opacity transitions at 150ms.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (section titles) + Libre Baskerville (body) + JetBrains Mono (data). Evokes a premium legal or financial document.
</text>

## Approach 3: Obsidian Vault — Dark Premium Dashboard
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Dark luxury SaaS — Notion meets Figma meets Private Equity portal

**Core Principles:**
- Deep dark background creates focus and reduces eye strain for long sessions
- Gold/amber accents on dark create premium, high-contrast hierarchy
- Card-based layout with subtle glass morphism
- Progress indicators and completion states are first-class citizens

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0D1B2A) base; dark card surfaces (#1B3A5C); gold (#C9A84C) for primary actions and highlights; white for primary text; muted grey for secondary text. The palette communicates: serious, premium, institutional.

**Layout Paradigm:** Left sidebar (fixed, collapsible) with unit navigation; main content area with a sticky header showing current unit + progress; tool content in structured card panels with tabbed sections.

**Signature Elements:**
- Gold left border on active/selected items
- Frosted glass cards with subtle border glow
- Animated progress ring showing overall toolkit completion

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, purposeful. Hover states reveal depth. Active states use gold glow. Completion checkboxes animate with a satisfying tick.

**Animation:** 250ms cubic-bezier transitions; sidebar slides; cards fade-scale in; checkboxes animate with a green pulse on completion.

**Typography System:** Cormorant Garamond (hero/display) + DM Sans (UI/body) + JetBrains Mono (financial data). Hierarchy creates clear distinction between editorial and functional content.
</text>

## CHOSEN APPROACH: Approach 3 — Obsidian Vault (Dark Premium Dashboard)

This design philosophy best serves the target audience: senior executives accustomed to Bloomberg terminals, private equity portals, and institutional-grade software. The dark premium aesthetic signals authority and seriousness, while the gold accents connect directly to the 120% brand palette established in the Excel workbook. The card-based layout with progress tracking is ideal for a multi-tool, multi-unit toolkit where clients need to navigate 30 tools across 10 units without feeling overwhelmed.
